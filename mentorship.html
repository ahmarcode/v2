My Mentoring Style

I strive to function as a catalyst and challenge you to grow as both a developer and a human. I care about the details and will encourage you to think, plan, and dream. These are qualities I will bring to every conversation.

Authenticity

No sugar-coated critiques here. I care about your growth too much to not keep it real. We'll tear things down and rebuild them better, together.

Humility

I don’t profess to be at the top of the developer talent pool, because I know there is still much for me to master. I.T is a moving target I'm hunting every day.

Equality

To me, you're more than just a mentee. You’re a developer — an industry peer. I’m not the only one teaching and you’re not the only one learning.

Transparency

I don’t have all the answers, but I’m happy to point you towards useful resources and share what I've learned from my own personal design journey.
